FNTD1 Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some answers to questions you might have for FNTD1.

Clean-Mode: Off
Ads help keep this site running.
Turn on Clean-Mode for an Ad-free experience.
Unit Obtainment Methods?
Various rare units are obtained through different summon types and presents, with specific drop rates ranging from 0.001% to 0.5%. Notable rates:
  • The Glitched — 0.01%
  • Toxic Springtrap — 0.001%
  • Queen Of Hearts Ballora — 0.005%
Will FNTD1 Get Updates?
After the last update (update 51), the game has not and will not receive updates anymore.
Best Enchant For My Units?
  • Slowers: Vengeance or Overclocked
  • Stunners: Overclocked
  • Endgame / DPS units with damage passives: Springlock recommended
What's Stun And True Stun?
Stun: Affects enemies only. Enemies have 0.8s immunity after being stunned.

True Stun: Affects enemies and bosses. Enemies have 5s immunity after being true stunned.
What Is The Meta?
All Metas are permanent except PvP, due to the game not receiving any more updates.

How Do I Transfer My Units?
There is no direct unit or item transfer system in FNTD1.
Is This Event Permanent?
Yes — the Summer 2 Event is permanent.
Is This Season Permanent?
Yes — Season 9 is permanent.
Will FNTD1 Be Deleted?
FNTD1 will never be privated or deleted.
